Kasey Tiller Photography

I got into photography because of my art. I was a very active artist from middle school all the way through high school. Unfortunately when I had my kids, I had to stop all of my art projects. There was never enough time to do anything except be a mom. With that said, the list of what I wanted to do never stopped growing. I would see a sunset that I wanted to paint, or I would see a street scape that would be amazing as a sketch, or even a portrait that I thought would look fantastic as a sketch or painting. I was extremely upset over a stagnant portfolio that never seemed to get looked at, let alone updated. Then I had an idea! Since I never had to time to slow down and work on my art the way I used to before the kids, I figured why not take a photo to come back to later!!? At the time I thought it was a save-all for my number one hobby/interest at the time, but little did I know it would lead me to one I might love even more.

Please view some of my shots below and please feel free to reach out to me if you are interested in any or would like to talk about a project that I might can help with.
